There are several companies that like Motec, Haltec, Link, AEm & several others that already make engine management systems. There are also several companies such as Cartek and Summit Racing that make Wireless Steeri8ng wheel control systems but (with the exception of Motec) there are no out of the box single supplier options that are affordable at a commercial level..
